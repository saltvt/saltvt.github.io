# Posting More Consistently. A Mental Framework 

I got asked to make a video on how to post a bit more consistently. So I figured I'd post it on the site. I've made a bunch of different types of content over the last year but I didn't really hit a stride until I sat down and categorized them. My content can be categorized into 4 categories:
- Mobile Screen Recordings(easiest)
- Trends/Sounds
- Scripted Content like this video 
- Skits(hardest)

## Mobile Screen Recordings
As i said before, scheduling a bunch of videos on a single day is better but if you miss a day opening vtube studio on your phone and screen recording a thought or sentiment is low effort and you can post it almost immediately.

## Trending Sounds and Trends 
The second thing is trending sounds. This is honestly the second easiest thing to do and I have a folder of them. Some of them do really well some not so much but the whole point of these is to be fun. These tend to do the best for me.

## Scripted And Edited Content
Scripted content imho is higher difficulty because I think of them as providing value for following and watching consistently. This could be highlights, an idea, or an actual clip from stream. These get pushed to followers the most but require scripting, editing, graphics and just a lot of effort. Irl comes first and life is an optimization problem so I limit these to 1 or 2 every couple of weeks.

## Skits 
These are the hardest and take the most time in my honest opinion. They tend to require the most creativity, focus, and editing. These generally get the most engagement when it comes to the algorithm's that social media sites use. 

Hopefully this frameworks helps someone with the organizational side of posting and content creation
